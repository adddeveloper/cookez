Top 10 Foods to Boost Your Immune System

  1. Citrus fruits

    High in vitamin C, which helps stimulate the production of white blood cells that fight infection.

  2. Ginger

    A natural anti-inflammatory that can help reduce inflammation and boost the immune system.

  3. Garlic

    Contains compounds that can help boost the immune system and has been shown to have antibacterial and antiviral properties.

  4. Almonds

    High in vitamin E, which can help boost the immune system and has antioxidant properties.

  5. Yogurt

    Contains probiotics that can help improve gut health, which is important for a strong immune system.

  6. Spinach

    High in vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants, which can help boost the immune system.

  7. Turmeric

    A natural anti-inflammatory that can help reduce inflammation and boost the immune system.

  8. Broccoli

    High in vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants, which can help boost the immune system.

  9. Green tea

    Contains antioxidants and compounds that can help boost the immune system and has been shown to have antiviral and antibacterial properties.

  10. Red bell peppers

    High in vitamin C, which can help stimulate the production of white blood cells that fight infection.
