Top Ten Authentic Street Foods from Around the World

  1. Tacos al pastor

    A Mexican street food made with marinated pork cooked on a vertical spit, served with pineapple, onion, cilantro, and lime in a soft corn tortilla.

  2. Banh mi

    A Vietnamese sandwich made with a crispy baguette filled with grilled meat, pickled vegetables, fresh cilantro, and spicy mayonnaise.

  3. Currywurst

    A German street food consisting of a grilled pork sausage sliced and smothered in a spicy tomato-based curry sauce, often served with fries or bread.

  4. Falafel

    A Middle Eastern street food made with ground chickpeas, herbs, and spices formed into balls or patties and fried, and served in a pita with tahini sauce and vegetables.

  5. Churros

    A Spanish dessert consisting of deep-fried dough pastry strips coated in cinnamon sugar, often served with a chocolate dipping sauce.

  6. Samosas

    An Indian street food made with crispy pastry filled with spiced vegetables, meat, or cheese, and often served with chutney or yogurt sauce.

  7. Poutine

    A Canadian dish made with crispy French fries, cheese curds, and gravy, often with added toppings such as bacon or smoked meat.

  8. Burek

    A Balkan street food made with flaky phyllo pastry filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables, and often served with yogurt or sour cream.

  9. Baozi

    A Chinese street food consisting of soft steamed buns filled with savory meat or vegetable fillings, and often eaten for breakfast or as a snack.

  10. Pupusas

    A Salvadoran street food made with thick corn tortillas filled with cheese, beans, or meat, and often served with a side of curtido (pickled cabbage salad) and salsa.
