Top Ten Underrated Food Cities Around the World

  1. Bologna, Italy

    Bologna is a food lover's paradise, with a rich culinary heritage and specialties like tortellini, mortadella, and ragu. Don't miss the outdoor markets and Osteria del Sole.

  2. Chiang Mai, Thailand

    Chiang Mai offers a unique and authentic Thai culinary experience, with specialties like khao soi, sai ua, and nam prik noom. Don't miss the night markets and street food stalls.

  3. La Paz, Bolivia

    La Paz is a hidden gem for food lovers, with a mix of indigenous and Spanish culinary traditions and specialties like salteñas, pique macho, and chairo soup. Don't miss the markets and local eateries.

  4. Portland, Maine, USA

    Portland, Maine is a coastal city with a thriving food scene, featuring fresh seafood, craft beer, and unique culinary experiences like lobster rolls and whoopie pies. Don't miss the food trucks and farm-to-table restaurants.

  5. Kathmandu, Nepal

    Kathmandu offers a diverse and flavorful cuisine, with influences from Tibetan, Indian, and Chinese cultures and specialties like momos, dal bhat, and samay baji. Don't miss the street food and local markets.

  6. Guadalajara, Mexico

    Guadalajara is a hidden foodie paradise, with a rich culinary heritage and specialties like birria, tequila, and tortas ahogadas. Don't miss the local markets and street food scene.

  7. Budapest, Hungary

    Budapest is a city of vibrant culture and cuisine, featuring specialties like goulash, chimney cakes, and lángos. Don't miss the local markets and traditional Hungarian restaurants.

  8. Goa, India

    Goa offers a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese influences, resulting in a diverse and flavorful cuisine with specialties like vindaloo, xacuti, and bebinca. Don't miss the seafood and local spice farms.

  9. Cartagena, Colombia

    Cartagena offers a fusion of Caribbean, African, and Spanish influences, resulting in a unique and flavorful cuisine with specialties like ceviche, arepas, and sancocho. Don't miss the street food and local markets.

  10. Chiang Rai, Thailand

    Chiang Rai is a hidden gem in Thailand, with a rich culinary scene featuring specialties like khao tom, khao soi nam ngiao, and nam phrik ong. Don't miss the local markets and hill tribe cuisine.
