Ten Cooking Techniques

  1. Grilling

    Grilling is the process of cooking food over an open flame or hot coals. It is a popular method for cooking meat, fish, and vegetables and can add a smoky, charred flavor to foods.

  2. Roasting

    Roasting is a dry-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in an oven or on a spit. It is commonly used for cooking meat, poultry, and vegetables and can result in a crispy, caramelized exterior and tender interior.

  3. Baking

    Baking is a dry-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in an oven using hot air. It is commonly used for cooking bread, cakes, pastries, and casseroles.

  4. Sautéing

    Sautéing is a dry-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in a small amount of oil or fat over high heat. It is commonly used for cooking vegetables, meat, and seafood and can result in a crisp, golden-brown exterior.

  5. Stir-frying

    Stir-frying is a dry-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in a small amount of oil or fat over high heat while stirring constantly. It is commonly used for cooking Asian-inspired dishes and can result in a crispy, flavorful dish.

  6. Boiling

    Boiling is a moist-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in boiling water or broth. It is commonly used for cooking pasta, rice, vegetables, and eggs.

  7. Steaming

    Steaming is a moist-heat cooking method that involves cooking food over, but not in, boiling water. It is commonly used for cooking vegetables, seafood, and dumplings and can result in a tender, flavorful dish.

  8. Poaching

    Poaching is a moist-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in a liquid, such as water, broth, or wine. It is commonly used for cooking eggs, fish, and poultry and can result in a tender, flavorful dish.

  9. Braising

    Braising is a combination cooking method that involves browning food in a small amount of fat and then simmering it in a liquid. It is commonly used for cooking meat and vegetables and can result in a tender, flavorful dish.

  10. Frying

    Frying is a dry-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in hot oil or fat. It is commonly used for cooking fried chicken, french fries, and doughnuts and can result in a crispy, golden-brown exterior.
