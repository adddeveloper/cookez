Top 10 Foods That Can Help Lower Your Cholesterol

  1. Oats

    Oats are high in soluble fiber, which can help lower LDL cholesterol (the "bad" cholesterol) levels.

  2. Nuts

    Nuts such as almonds, walnuts, and pistachios are high in healthy fats and fiber, which can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  3. Fatty fish

    Fatty fish such as salmon, mackerel, and sardines are high in omega-3 fatty acids, which can help lower triglycerides and increase HDL cholesterol (the "good" cholesterol) levels.

  4. Beans and legumes

    Beans and legumes such as chickpeas, lentils, and kidney beans are high in soluble fiber and protein, which can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  5. Avoid trans fats

    Avoiding trans fats found in processed foods, fried foods, and baked goods can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  6. Avocado

    Avocado is high in healthy fats and fiber, which can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  7. Fruits and berries

    Fruits and berries such as apples, strawberries, and blueberries are high in soluble fiber and antioxidants, which can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  8. Veggies

    Vegetables such as Brussels sprouts, broccoli, and sweet potatoes are high in fiber, which can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  9. Green tea

    Green tea is high in antioxidants and may help lower LDL cholesterol levels.

  10. Soy

    Soy products such as tofu and edamame are high in protein and can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.
