Top Ten Meal Ideas for Kids that Are Actually Healthy

  1. Vegetable and hummus wrap

    A wrap made with whole-grain tortillas, hummus, and a variety of colorful vegetables like carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers.

  2. Chicken and vegetable skewers

    Skewers made with grilled chicken and a variety of vegetables such as zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and mushrooms.

  3. Homemade pizza with vegetables

    A healthy pizza made with a whole-grain crust, tomato sauce, and a variety of vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms.

  4. Tuna salad with crackers

    A protein-packed tuna salad made with Greek yogurt and served with whole-grain crackers or sliced vegetables for dipping.

  5. Vegetable and bean quesadillas

    A tasty and filling quesadilla made with a whole-grain tortilla, black beans, and a variety of vegetables like onions, bell peppers, and corn.

  6. Homemade chicken nuggets with sweet potato fries

    Healthy chicken nuggets made with baked chicken breast and served with sweet potato fries, a great source of vitamin A.

  7. Grilled cheese with tomato soup

    A classic grilled cheese sandwich made with whole-grain bread and served with a homemade tomato soup made with fresh tomatoes.

  8. Spaghetti with turkey meatballs

    A classic spaghetti and meatballs dish made with ground turkey instead of beef and served with a homemade tomato sauce.

  9. Quinoa salad with fruit and nuts

    A refreshing and nutritious salad made with quinoa, mixed fruits, and chopped nuts for added crunch.

  10. Homemade chicken fajitas

    A flavorful and colorful meal made with grilled chicken and a variety of vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes, served with whole-grain tortillas.
