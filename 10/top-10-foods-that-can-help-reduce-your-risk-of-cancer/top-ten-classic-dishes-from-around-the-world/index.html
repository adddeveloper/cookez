Top Ten Classic Dishes from Around the World

  1. Pizza Margherita

    A classic Italian pizza made with a thin crust, tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves.

  2. Paella

    A Spanish rice dish made with saffron, a variety of seafood and/or meats, and vegetables.

  3. Pad Thai

    A Thai stir-fried noodle dish made with rice noodles, vegetables, tofu, and peanuts, flavored with tamarind sauce and lime juice.

  4. Biryani

    A fragrant Indian rice dish made with spices, vegetables, and meat or seafood.

  5. Beef bourguignon

    A French beef stew made with red wine, mushrooms, onions, and bacon.

  6. Sushi

    A Japanese dish made with vinegared rice and various seafood, vegetables, and egg.

  8. Chili con carne

    A Tex-Mex dish made with chili peppers, ground beef, tomatoes, and spices.

  9. Chicken tikka masala

    A popular British-Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a creamy tomato sauce flavored with spices.

  10. Pho

    A Vietnamese noodle soup made with beef or chicken broth, rice noodles, and various herbs and spices.
