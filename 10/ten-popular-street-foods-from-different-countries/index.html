Top Ten Popular Street Foods from Different Countries

  • Banh Mi - Vietnam

    A Vietnamese sandwich made with a baguette, pickled veggies, cilantro, chili, and various meat fillings.

  • Poutine - Canada

    A dish made with French fries, cheese curds, and gravy. Variations include toppings like bacon and fried eggs.

  • Empanadas - Argentina

    A pastry filled with meat, cheese, vegetables, or fruit, then baked or fried.

  • Tacos - Mexico

    A soft or hard shell filled with meat, cheese, beans, lettuce, and salsa. Variations include fish and vegetarian options.

  • Currywurst - Germany

    Sliced sausage topped with curry ketchup and served with fries.

  • Arepa - Venezuela

    A flatbread made of maize dough, filled with meat, cheese, avocado, and other toppings.

  • Churros - Spain

    A fried dough pastry rolled in sugar and served with a dipping sauce like chocolate or caramel.

  • Baklava - Turkey

    A sweet pastry made of layers of phyllo dough filled with chopped nuts and honey syrup.

  • Satay - Indonesia

    Skewers of grilled meat (usually chicken or beef) served with peanut sauce and rice.

  • Samosa - India

    A fried or baked pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and other vegetables or meat.
