Top Ten Best Food Restaurants in the World

  1. Osteria Francescana, Modena, Italy

    Osteria Francescana is a three-Michelin-star restaurant that serves modern Italian cuisine with a creative twist. The chef, Massimo Bottura, is known for his innovative and playful approach to cooking.

  2. El Celler de Can Roca, Girona, Spain

    El Celler de Can Roca is a three-Michelin-star restaurant that serves modern Catalan cuisine. The Roca brothers, who run the restaurant, are known for their commitment to sustainability and use of local ingredients.

  3. Mirazur, Menton, France

    Mirazur is a three-Michelin-star restaurant that serves contemporary French cuisine with a focus on seafood. Chef Mauro Colagreco sources many of his ingredients from his own garden.

  4. Gaggan, Bangkok, Thailand

    Gaggan is a progressive Indian restaurant that combines traditional Indian flavors with modern techniques. Chef Gaggan Anand has been named Asia's Best Chef multiple times.

  5. Central, Lima, Peru

    Central is a restaurant that celebrates the biodiversity of Peru through its cuisine. Chef Virgilio Martinez is known for his use of indigenous ingredients and his commitment to preserving Peruvian culinary traditions.

  6. Maido, Lima, Peru

    Maido is a restaurant that serves Nikkei cuisine, a fusion of Japanese and Peruvian flavors. Chef Mitsuharu Tsumura is known for his creativity and use of fresh ingredients.

  7. Arpège, Paris, France

    Arpège is a three-Michelin-star restaurant that serves French cuisine with a focus on vegetables. Chef Alain Passard is known for his vegetable-centric approach and use of organic produce from his own farm.

  8. Mugaritz, San Sebastian, Spain

    Mugaritz is a two-Michelin-star restaurant that serves avant-garde Basque cuisine. Chef Andoni Luis Aduriz is known for his experimental and creative approach to cooking.

  9. Eleven Madison Park, New York City, USA

    Eleven Madison Park is a three-Michelin-star restaurant that serves modern American cuisine. Chef Daniel Humm is known for his use of local and seasonal ingredients and his commitment to sustainability.

  10. Narisawa, Tokyo, Japan

    Narisawa is a restaurant that serves contemporary Japanese cuisine with a focus on sustainability. Chef Yoshihiro Narisawa is known for his use of locally sourced ingredients and his commitment to preserving Japan's culinary heritage.
