Top 10 Foods That Can Help Lower Your Blood Pressure

  1. Leafy greens

    Leafy greens such as spinach and kale are high in potassium and can help lower blood pressure.

  2. Berries

    Berries such as strawberries and blueberries are high in antioxidants and can help lower blood pressure.

  3. Oatmeal

    Oatmeal is high in fiber and can help lower blood pressure.

  4. Yogurt

    Yogurt is high in calcium and can help lower blood pressure.

  5. Bananas

    Bananas are high in potassium and can help lower blood pressure.

  6. Salmon

    Salmon is high in omega-3 fatty acids and can help lower blood pressure.

  7. Seeds

    Seeds such as pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds are high in magnesium and can help lower blood pressure.

  8. Garlic

    Garlic can help lower blood pressure by relaxing blood vessels.

  9. Dark chocolate

    Dark chocolate is high in flavonoids and can help lower blood pressure.

  10. Tomatoes

    Tomatoes are high in potassium and can help lower blood pressure.
